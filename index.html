Technology and My Team

Simplifying the story quite a bit, I worked with Robert and Clay exploring the broader vision of what Stonegate's infrastructure needed and what we could do to scale all of the company's applications along side this high visibilty patient quality improvement project. We decided on a 5 node Nutanix cluster hosted at DataBank, a co-located data center in north Dallas. Nutanix is linux based, similar to our existing data warehouse, business intelligence and data ETL (Extract, Transform, and Load) environment. Nutanix was designed to accomodate the distributed, microservice architecture I planned for the project. Concretely, we used Docker to containerize our project components and tools including:

  • MariaDB (MySQL)- staging data
  • Vertica- analytic queries
  • Pentaho Data Integration (Kettle)visual ETL tool
  • Pentaho Server- dashboards, reports, "the application"
